# Brown Biotech Role Deliverables

## 1. Founder / Strategy
**Deliverables:**
- primary motion decision
- approval decisions
- lane priorities
- tradeoff log

## 2. Intake PM
**Deliverables:**
- clean request ID
- completed intake record
- first-response quality
- handoff completeness

## 3. Triage Lead
**Deliverables:**
- fit score
- urgency score
- approval risk flag
- route recommendation

## 4. Routing Lead
**Deliverables:**
- lane assignment
- owner assignment
- next action
- escalation path

## 5. Peptide-Service Lead
**Deliverables:**
- scoped paid brief
- quote path
- consultation path
- delivery receipt

## 6. Biostatx Lead
**Deliverables:**
- analysis brief
- reporting plan
- data interpretation summary
- decision-support note

## 7. Genox-Site Lead
**Deliverables:**
- discovery framing memo
- partner scoping note
- collaboration route
- follow-up summary

## 8. Business-Pipeline Lead
**Deliverables:**
- routing improvements
- workflow automation
- intake system refinements
- reusable ops template

## 9. Proof / Publications Lead
**Deliverables:**
- short proof note
- case note
- publication draft
- partner update

## 10. QA / Approval Lead
**Deliverables:**
- claim check
- risk check
- consistency check
- launch check

## Quality rule
Each deliverable should answer one question:
- What is it?
- Who owns it?
- What happens next?
- Does it need approval?
