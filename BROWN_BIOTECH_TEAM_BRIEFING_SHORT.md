# Brown Biotech Team Briefing — Short Version

**We turn inbound interest into a paid brief, route it cleanly, and publish proof that makes the next brief easier to close.**

- Primary motion: `Paid Brief → peptide-service`
- Team: 10 roles, one owner per live item
- Weekly loop: intake → triage → route → deliver → proof → review → decide
- Rule: if it does not improve intake, routing, delivery, proof, or review, do not add it

## Meeting output
- one owner per item
- one next action per item
- one risk per item
- one due date per item
