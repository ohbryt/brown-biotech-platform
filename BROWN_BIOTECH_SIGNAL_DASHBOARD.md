# Brown Biotech Signal Dashboard

## Purpose
Show the daily signal pipeline in one screen:
- what changed
- why it matters
- what to do next

## Dashboard sections
### 1) Today’s top signals
- FDA regulatory / access / AI-platform updates
- biotech deal flow and funding
- manufacturing / supply-chain pressure
- AI-drug-design and peptide/CDMO news

### 2) Brown relevance filters
Score each item:
- peptide demand
- licensing / partnership signal
- regulatory / funding shift

### 3) Output ladder
- **Act** — immediate outreach or proof update
- **Refine** — keep watching, maybe convert to weekly review
- **Archive** — low relevance or generic biotech noise

### 4) Receipts
Each item should be captured with:
- source
- headline
- date
- URL
- relevance score
- why it matters

### 5) Next action box
Always end with one concrete action:
- outreach target
- publication angle
- routing follow-up
- approval follow-up

## Recommended layout
- Top left: current date and status
- Top right: decision badge
- Middle: 3–5 live signal cards
- Bottom left: outreach list
- Bottom right: next action

## Source families
- FDA press announcements
- Endpoints News
- Fierce Biotech
- Major AI / peptide company newsrooms
- Nature Biotechnology when accessible

## Rule
Do not build a dashboard that only shows headlines.
It must show the decision attached to the headline.
