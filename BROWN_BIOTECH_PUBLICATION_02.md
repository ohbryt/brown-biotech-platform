# Brown Biotech Publication 02

## Title
A daily signal is only useful if it becomes an outreach list

## One-line takeaway
The value of a biotech signal memo is not the memo itself; it is the next action it creates.

## Why this matters
A lot of teams collect articles, but they do not convert them into decisions.
That creates activity without motion.

Brown Biotech’s daily signal workflow is designed to do three things:
- verify a live headline
- score its Brown relevance
- convert it into an owner and next step

## What changed
We moved from a source-family map to a live article-level workflow.
That means each item now needs:
- source
- headline
- date
- URL
- relevance score
- why it matters

## Why it matters for Brown Biotech
This is how the peptide-service lane stays focused.
It also helps the team distinguish between generic biotech news and real outreach opportunities.

## Next step
Use the signal dashboard to generate a weekly outreach list for AI-drug platform teams, peptide/CDMO targets, and fast-moving discovery groups.
