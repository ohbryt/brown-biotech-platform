# Brown Biotech Execution Checklist

## Daily
- Check new requests.
- Triage fit and urgency.
- Assign one owner.
- Confirm whether human approval is needed.
- Write the next action into the hub.

## Weekly
- Review wins.
- Review blockers.
- Review risks.
- Review open requests.
- Decide what gets promoted, merged, or retired.

## Launch checks
- Website copy matches the HQ language.
- Intake receipt shows request ID, route, owner, approval gate, and next action.
- Notion hub links are current.
- Primary lane is still peptide-service.
- Research proof and publication signals are visible.
- No high-stakes item ships without review.

## Decision rule
If it does not help the paid-brief motion, remove it or move it lower in priority.
