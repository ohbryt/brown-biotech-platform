# Brown Biotech Team Operating Slide

## Title
Brown Biotech 10-Person Team Operating System

## One-line strategy
Turn inbound interest into a paid brief, route it cleanly, deliver it fast, and publish proof that improves the next brief.

## Primary motion
**Paid Brief → peptide-service**

## Team design
- Founder / Strategy
- Intake PM
- Triage Lead
- Routing Lead
- Peptide-Service Lead
- Biostatx Lead
- Genox-Site Lead
- Business-Pipeline Lead
- Proof / Publications Lead
- QA / Approval Lead

## Weekly loop
1. Intake
2. Triage
3. Route
4. Deliver
5. Proof
6. Review
7. Decide

## 30-day target
- one stable intake flow
- one stable paid-brief path
- one proof stream
- one partner funnel
- one weekly review cadence

## Risks
- too many lanes
- free-consulting drift
- unclear owners
- weak proof reuse
- slow approval gates

## Decision rule
If a task does not improve intake, routing, delivery, proof, or review, do not add it.
