### Why this paper matters for Brown Biotech

scGPT is a generative pretrained transformer trained on **33 million human cells** (CELLxGENE, 51 organs, 441 studies). It enables 5 downstream tasks: cell type annotation, multi-batch integration, multi-omic integration, **perturbation response prediction**, and gene regulatory network inference.

### Key benchmark results
- **Cell type annotation:** Outperforms TOSICA, scBERT (hPancreas, ~0.85 accuracy)
- **Perturbation prediction:** Outperforms GEARS by 5-20% margins (Adamson, Norman, Replogle datasets)
- **Multi-omic integration:** Only method that successfully integrates RNA + ATAC + protein with batch correction
- **Cross-tissue transfer:** PBMC 10k works **without fine-tuning** (highlights pretraining generalizability)
