# Brown Biotech Proof and Partner Plan

## Goal
Make Brown Biotech easier to trust, easier to understand, and easier to partner with.

## Borrowed pattern
- Show proof publicly.
- Keep the community warm.
- Make partnership a routed process, not an open-ended chat.

## What to publish
- Selected service briefs
- Short research summaries
- Case-study style proof notes
- Weekly updates when they create trust or momentum

## What to route
- Partner interest
- Collaboration requests
- Advisory requests
- Commercial leads

## Operating rule
If a request has potential value, route it into a brief, one owner, and one next action.
