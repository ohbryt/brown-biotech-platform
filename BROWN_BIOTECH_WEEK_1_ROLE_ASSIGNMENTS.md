# Brown Biotech Week 1 Role Assignments

## Goal
Make week 1 execution explicit by role.

| Role | Week 1 focus | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Founder / Strategy | lock primary motion and approvals | decision memo |
| Intake PM | fix intake questions and request ID flow | intake spec |
| Triage Lead | define fit and urgency scoring | triage rules |
| Routing Lead | define lane-to-owner mapping | route map |
| Peptide-Service Lead | draft the first paid brief flow | brief template |
| Biostatx Lead | define analysis/report request shape | analysis brief template |
| Genox-Site Lead | define partner-scoping request shape | partner note template |
| Business-Pipeline Lead | map routing and automation tasks | workflow map |
| Proof / Publications Lead | draft 1–2 proof items | publication draft |
| QA / Approval Lead | define checks for risky items | approval checklist |

## Week 1 deliverable rule
Each role must produce one short artifact that can be used immediately in Notion or on the site.

## Completion standard
Week 1 is done when the team can process one request from intake to receipt without ambiguity.
