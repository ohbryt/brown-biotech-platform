# Brown Biotech 10-Person Team Briefing

## One-line brief
We are turning inbound interest into a paid brief, routing it cleanly, and publishing proof that makes the next brief easier to close.

## Primary motion
**Paid Brief → peptide-service**

## Team structure
- Founder / Strategy
- Intake PM
- Triage Lead
- Routing Lead
- Peptide-Service Lead
- Biostatx Lead
- Genox-Site Lead
- Business-Pipeline Lead
- Proof / Publications Lead
- QA / Approval Lead

## Weekly loop
1. Intake
2. Triage
3. Route
4. Deliver
5. Proof
6. Review
7. Decide

## What each week should produce
- one stable intake path
- one stable paid-brief path
- one proof item
- one partner signal
- one decision log entry

## Operating rules
- Use the same words everywhere.
- Keep the primary lane first.
- Keep high-stakes items under human approval.
- Do not expand lanes unless they reduce friction.

## Meeting outcome
Every meeting must end with:
- one owner per live item
- one next action per live item
- one risk per live item
- one due date per live item
