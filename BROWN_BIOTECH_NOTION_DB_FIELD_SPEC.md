# Brown Biotech Notion DB Field Spec

## 1. Intake Requests
**Purpose:** track every inbound request.

**Fields:**
- Request ID (title)
- Created date
- Name
- Company / project
- Contact
- Service lane
- Request summary
- Problem
- Desired outcome
- Timeline
- Budget range
- Triage
- Fit score
- Route
- Owner
- Approval needed
- Next action
- Status
- Last touch
- Source

## 2. Intake Routing
**Purpose:** keep routing logic visible.

**Fields:**
- Route (title)
- Lane
- Fit level
- Owner
- Escalation path
- Reason
- Updated at

## 3. Funnel
**Purpose:** manage lead stage and follow-up.

**Fields:**
- Lead name (title)
- Stage
- Source
- Lane
- Owner
- Last touch
- Next action
- Qualification
- Approval needed

## 4. Weekly Reviews
**Purpose:** capture decisions and progress.

**Fields:**
- Week (title)
- Wins
- Blockers
- Risks
- Decisions
- Next week priorities

## 5. Decision Log
**Purpose:** store important choices.

**Fields:**
- Date (title or date)
- Decision
- Why
- Owner
- Impact
- Follow-up

## 6. Research Proof
**Purpose:** store proof assets.

**Fields:**
- Title
- Type
- Lane
- One-line takeaway
- Source / citation
- Date
- Use in sales? (checkbox)

## 7. Publications
**Purpose:** track draft and published proof.

**Fields:**
- Title
- Publication lane
- Status
- Takeaway
- Next step

## 8. Partner Funnel
**Purpose:** manage partner-related leads.

**Fields:**
- Contact / org
- Signal
- Qualify
- Brief
- Route
- Review
- Deliver
- Owner

## 9. Team Roster
**Purpose:** define the 10-person team.

**Fields:**
- Role (title)
- Owner
- Scope
- Main deliverables
- KPI

## 10. Team Strategy
**Purpose:** keep the strategy current.

**Fields:**
- Objective (title)
- Primary motion
- Supporting lanes
- 30-day target
- Risks
- Decision needed

## 11. Team Operating Pack
**Purpose:** keep the weekly execution system together.

**Fields:**
- Week
- Agenda
- KPI review
- Decision log
- Next strategy
