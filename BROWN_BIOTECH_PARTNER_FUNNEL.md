# Brown Biotech Partner Funnel

## Goal
Turn serious interest into a brief, an owner, and a next action.

## Funnel stages
1. **Signal** — someone asks, refers, or shows interest.
2. **Qualify** — check fit, scope, and urgency.
3. **Brief** — move the conversation into a paid brief or scoped note.
4. **Route** — assign lane and owner.
5. **Review** — human approval for high-stakes items.
6. **Deliver** — send the receipt, memo, or next step.

## Intake fields
- Who is reaching out?
- What problem are they trying to solve?
- Which lane fits best?
- Is this a brief, a partnership, or a referral?
- What is the first next action?
- Is human approval needed?

## Rules
- Do not keep partner interest in a loose chat.
- If the fit is weak, say so early.
- If the fit is real, convert to a brief.
- Every serious lead gets one owner.

## Receipt rules
- Always return route, owner, and next action.
- Keep the receipt short and shareable.
- If the fit is weak, close the loop cleanly instead of keeping it in chat.

## Data source
- The dashboard should read from saved daily briefs in `Daily-Briefs/` so the weekly loop can review real outputs, not only prompts.
