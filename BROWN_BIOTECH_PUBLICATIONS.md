# Brown Biotech Publications Plan

## Goal
Publish enough proof to build trust without turning the site into a blog farm.

## Publication lanes
- **Research brief** — decision-ready summary with citations and a clear recommendation.
- **Case note** — short proof note showing how a problem was framed and routed.
- **Methods note** — reusable process note for how the work was done.
- **Partner update** — short public signal for collaborators and serious inbound leads.

## What to publish
- Selected briefs with a concrete outcome
- Compact case-study style notes
- Evidence summaries that support the primary lane
- Updates that make partnership easier, not noisier

## Rules
- Keep it short.
- Keep it credible.
- Tie each piece to one decision or one offer.
- Do not publish anything that weakens a high-stakes review gate.

## Output format
- Title
- One-line takeaway
- What was done
- Why it matters
- Next step
