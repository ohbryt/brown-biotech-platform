About us

On-Dining (온-다이닝) is a Brown Biotech pilot service connecting older adults who want company at meals with neighborhood restaurants and matching companions. Built on the same System-of-Intelligence infrastructure as our peptide and research lanes, On-Dining turns a verified care-matching engine into a real-world product — currently piloting in Gwangju, Korea with partner restaurants and senior community centers.

What we offer

Verified meal companions

Trained companions matched by language, mobility, and meal preference. Background-checked, Korean-speaking, and matched within 48 hours of request.

Restaurant partner network

Vetted neighborhood restaurants with senior-friendly menus, soft-texture options, and group seating. Gwangju pilot: 12 partner venues across 4 districts.

Family dashboard

Real-time updates for adult children: companion arrival, meal completion, mood check-in. Optional photo proof and weekly summary email.

Care-coordinator escalation

Automatic routing to Brown Biotech care ops if mood or mobility signals indicate the participant needs follow-up. HIPAA-aligned Korean PIPA compliance.

Frequently asked questions

Is this only for seniors living alone?

No. On-Dining serves anyone who wants company at meals — widowed spouses, recent movers, adult children seeking陪伴 for parents. Most pilot participants are 65+.

What does it cost?

Pilot is free through 2026 Q4. Post-pilot pricing: ₩12,000–18,000 per meal session including companion and a main course. Family members can pre-pay for 10-session bundles.

Where is this available?

Gwangju metropolitan area only during pilot (4 districts: Buk-gu, Nam-gu, Seo-gu, Gwangsan-gu). Seoul launch planned 2027 Q1 if pilot metrics hit.

How is this related to biotech?

On-Dining is a pilot for Brown Biotech's verified matching engine — the same trust infrastructure that powers peptide-client routing. We're testing the engine in a high-touch consumer context before extending it to biotech clients.
