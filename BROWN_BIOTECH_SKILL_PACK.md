# Brown Biotech Skill Pack

A small reusable operating pack inspired by large skill libraries, but cut down to the pieces Brown Biotech actually uses.

## Core skills

### 1) Intake capture
Turn a message into a structured intake record.

### 2) Triage
Score fit, urgency, and approval risk.

### 3) Routing
Assign lane, owner, and next action.

### 4) Brief drafting
Write a paid brief or sample brief.

### 5) Source mapping
List the evidence, sources, or inputs behind the answer.

### 6) Approval gate
Hold risky items for human review.

### 7) Receipt formatting
Give the buyer a reusable, shareable artifact.

### 8) Launch check
Verify copy, routes, forms, notifications, and build state before publishing.

### 9) Weekly review
Summarize wins, blockers, risks, and next actions.

### 10) Spec-to-task conversion
Turn a brief into a short implementation plan.

## Reuse rule
Do not add more skills unless they remove real friction.
If a new skill does not improve intake, routing, delivery, or review, keep it out.
