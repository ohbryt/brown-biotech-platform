# Brown Biotech Team Roster v1

## Team purpose
A 10-person virtual team to turn inbound interest into paid briefs, proof assets, and routed delivery.

## Core rule
One brief, one owner, one next action.

## 10 roles
1. **Founder / Strategy**
   - Owns positioning, priorities, approvals, and final tradeoffs.
   - Output: strategic decisions.

2. **Intake PM**
   - Owns form quality, first response, request ID, and handoff completeness.
   - Output: structured intake records.

3. **Triage Lead**
   - Scores fit, urgency, and approval risk.
   - Output: hot/warm/cold plus route.

4. **Routing Lead**
   - Assigns lane, owner, and next action.
   - Output: clean route map.

5. **Peptide-Service Lead**
   - Owns the primary lane and paid-brief conversion.
   - Output: scoped briefs and quotes.

6. **Biostatx Lead**
   - Owns analysis, reporting, and decision support.
   - Output: analysis briefs and reports.

7. **Genox-Site Lead**
   - Owns discovery framing and partner scoping.
   - Output: partner-ready scope notes.

8. **Business-Pipeline Lead**
   - Owns routing logic, automation, and ops backbone.
   - Output: reusable workflow improvements.

9. **Proof / Publications Lead**
   - Owns short proof notes, case notes, and publication drafts.
   - Output: credibility assets.

10. **QA / Approval Lead**
    - Checks claims, risk, consistency, and launch readiness.
    - Output: approval gate and launch checks.

## Operating rhythm
- **Daily:** intake → triage → route → owner → next action
- **Weekly:** wins, blockers, risks, decisions, next week priorities
- **Monthly:** simplify lanes, retire weak offers, publish proof

## Team success condition
The team works when every inbound request leaves with:
- a lane
- an owner
- an approval decision if needed
- a receipt the buyer can reuse
