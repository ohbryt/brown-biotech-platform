# Brown Biotech Team Operating Pack

## Purpose
Give the 10-person team a single working system for weekly execution, KPI review, Notion structure, and decision logging.

## Weekly agenda (60 min)
### 1) Status check — 5 min
- What changed since last week?
- What is the current primary lane?
- What is blocked?

### 2) Intake review — 10 min
- New requests
- Fit score / urgency
- Route / owner / next action
- Approval flags

### 3) Lane review — 15 min
- peptide-service
- biostatx
- genox-site
- business-pipeline

For each lane:
- what came in
- what was delivered
- what should be reused
- what should be retired

### 4) Proof review — 10 min
- publications drafted or published
- case notes
- partner signals
- reusable proof assets

### 5) Decision review — 10 min
- what was decided
- why it was decided
- what changed in the system

### 6) Next-week lock — 10 min
- one owner per live item
- one next action per live item
- one risk per live item
- one outcome expected by next week

## KPI set
### Founder / Strategy
- primary motion clarity
- approval latency
- number of priority changes

### Intake PM
- response time
- completion rate of intake fields
- number of incomplete submissions

### Triage Lead
- triage turnaround time
- fit-score accuracy
- number of misrouted requests

### Routing Lead
- route accuracy
- owner assignment completeness
- next-action completeness

### Peptide-Service Lead
- briefs started
- briefs completed
- brief-to-delivery conversion

### Biostatx Lead
- analysis briefs completed
- report turnaround time
- decision-support usefulness

### Genox-Site Lead
- partner-scoped notes completed
- qualified partner leads
- partner handoff quality

### Business-Pipeline Lead
- routing improvements shipped
- automation steps reduced
- recurring workflow issues removed

### Proof / Publications Lead
- proof notes published
- publication drafts completed
- proof assets reused in sales

### QA / Approval Lead
- approval review time
- blocked high-stakes items
- launch check pass rate

## 30-day next strategy
### Week 1
- lock the team roles
- lock the weekly agenda
- lock the HQ page order

### Week 2
- measure intake and routing speed
- identify repeated request patterns
- remove ambiguous handoffs

### Week 3
- publish proof assets
- improve partner funnel copy
- reduce internal friction

### Week 4
- simplify anything unused
- merge overlapping actions
- keep only what helps intake, routing, delivery, proof, or review

## Operating rule
If a task does not improve intake, routing, delivery, proof, or review, it does not belong in the system.
