# Brown Biotech Notion HQ Page Map

## Purpose
This is the canonical Notion structure for Brown Biotech.
It keeps the company on a **paid brief → routing → delivery → review** motion.

## Root page
# Brown Biotech HQ

Top block order:
1. Callout: `One brief. One owner. One next action.`
2. Short company summary
3. Active lanes
4. Human approval rules
5. Quick links
6. Operating pages
7. Databases
8. Weekly control loop

## Canonical service lanes
Keep these names identical everywhere:
- peptide-service
- biostatx
- genox-site
- business-pipeline

## Human approval rules
Always require review for:
- money / spend
- legal / contract
- deployment
- clinical or medical claims
- public announcements
- any high-stakes partnership decision

## Weekly control loop
At minimum, answer:
- What came in?
- What was routed?
- What shipped?
- What blocked?
- What should become a reusable template?
