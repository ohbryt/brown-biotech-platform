# Brown Biotech Partner Funnel Copy

## Hero
**Turn interest into a brief, an owner, and a next action.**

Brown Biotech routes serious interest through a paid brief so every request becomes clear, scoped, and actionable.

**Primary CTA:** Request a Brief

## Subhead
If the fit is real, we route it. If it is not, we say so early.

## Funnel stages
1. **Signal** — someone reaches out, refers, or shows interest.
2. **Qualify** — we check fit, scope, and urgency.
3. **Brief** — the conversation becomes a paid brief or scoped note.
4. **Route** — the request gets a lane and an owner.
5. **Review** — high-stakes items get human approval.
6. **Deliver** — the receipt, memo, or next step goes out.

## Intake questions
- Who is reaching out?
- What problem are they trying to solve?
- Which lane is the best fit?
- Is this a brief, a partnership, or a referral?
- What is the first next action?
- Does this need human approval?

## Receipt copy
Your request was received, routed, and assigned a next action.

## No-fit copy
Thanks — this is not the right lane for us right now. If it becomes a brief or a better fit later, we can revisit.

## Trust copy
- Premium, concise, and proof-led
- Built for serious inbound, not open-ended chat
- Clear ownership from first contact
- Human approval stays on the high-stakes path

## Multimodal evidence copy
Brown Biotech routes the request *with* its evidence stack, not text alone.

- Upload the file, screenshot, report, or note that supports the brief.
- If a key artifact is missing, we ask for it before delivery.
- The receipt should always show the route, owner, and next action.
