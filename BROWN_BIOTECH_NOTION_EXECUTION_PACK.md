# Brown Biotech Notion Execution Pack

## Goal
This pack converts Brown Biotech HQ into an executable Notion operating system:
- paid brief intake
- routing
- delivery
- weekly review
- human approval gates
- reusable templates

## 1) HQ page copy

### Title
**Brown Biotech HQ**

### Top callout
`One brief. One owner. One next action.`

### Intro paragraph
Brown Biotech runs on a paid-brief motion. Every request enters through a visible intake path, gets routed to one owner, and ends in a decision-ready handoff or a clear no.
