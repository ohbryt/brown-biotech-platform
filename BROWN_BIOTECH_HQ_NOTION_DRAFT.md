# Brown Biotech HQ — Notion Draft

## Status
Business track resumed. Brown Biotech runs on a paid-brief motion: one brief, one owner, one next action.

## Company summary
Brown Biotech sells decision-ready biotech work through a paid brief, then routes each request into the right lane with a clear owner and human review when needed.

## Operating modes
- **Brief** → intake, qualification, routing
- **Discovery** → scoping, research framing, partner fit
- **Delivery** → execution, artifacts, service output
- **Review** → human approval, QA, sensitive decisions
- **Track** → receipt, follow-through, weekly review
- **Improve** → update the harness, templates, and wording from outcomes

## Operating rules
- Use the same lane names everywhere.
- Keep the website, intake form, and Notion hub aligned.
- Keep human approval on money, legal, contract, deployment, public announcement, and medical-claims decisions.
- If the fit is weak, say so early and route cleanly.
- Make the trust layer visible: inspectable workflow, privacy-aware execution, decision-ready output, and no blind automation.
